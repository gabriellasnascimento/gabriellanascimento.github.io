About Me

I am a Babson College student pursuing a degree in Business with a concentration in Legal Studies, originally from Rio de Janeiro and currently based in Boston. I am interested in how strategy, operations, and legal frameworks work together to support long-term business and investment decisions, particularly in entrepreneurial and advisory environments.

Through my academic and consulting experiences, I have worked across sustainability-focused ventures and consumer brands, gaining exposure to structured problem-solving, operational thinking, and strategic decision-making. I am motivated by environments that value clarity, discipline, and execution.

Looking Ahead
